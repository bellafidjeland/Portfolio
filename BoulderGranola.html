Boulder Granola

Packaging & Visual Identity

Boulder Granola is a food company that offers healthy and homemade granola. Their granola is made using a family recipe offering good taste and serious nutrition. For this school project my goal was to develop the company's visual identity and packaging. My challenge was to create a visual identity that would tell their story and origins from the mountains and represent the nature of Colorado. During the process I created my design to both functionally and emotionally change people's relationships with nutrition. I wanted to create a natural brand design that displays the beautiful nature of Colorado that aligns with the company's values.

For this project, I created a series of packaging that captures attention on the shelves with its visual language that sets it apart from the competition and represents what Boulder is already known for; a very health conscious city known for quality food. The design system consists of a colorful pattern inspired by the Flatirons.

The result of the design is an expressive brand poised to be a beloved choice for active and healthy consumers. It counteracts the boring cues of the category and celebrates the beautiful nature of Colorado and Boulder Granola's delicious family recipe.
